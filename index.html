Why Signage Compliance Matters in India

Legal Safety Requirements

Ensure your building meets mandatory safety signage requirements as per NBCC guidelines and BIS standards to avoid legal penalties and ensure occupant safety.

Life Safety Standards

Proper fire exit signage following IS 9457 and IS 12349 standards can save lives during emergencies by providing clear evacuation guidance.

Professional Credibility

Demonstrate your commitment to safety standards and regulatory compliance to clients, authorities, and stakeholders.

Essential Signage Compliance Applications

Commercial Buildings

Office complexes, shopping malls, and commercial spaces require comprehensive safety signage including fire exits, emergency assembly points, and directional guidance as per NBCC norms.

  • Fire exit and emergency signage (IS 9457)
  • Directional and wayfinding signs
  • Floor identification and room numbering

Healthcare Facilities

Hospitals and clinics need specialized signage for patient safety, infection control, and emergency procedures following medical facility guidelines.

  • Emergency exit routes for patient evacuation
  • Medical equipment and hazard identification
  • Infection control and hygiene signage

Construction Sites

Active construction zones require safety boards, hazard warnings, and protective equipment reminders to ensure worker safety and regulatory compliance.

  • Construction site safety boards
  • PPE requirement signage
  • Hazard identification and warning signs

Educational Institutions

Schools, colleges, and universities need age-appropriate safety signage, emergency evacuation plans, and clear directional guidance for students and staff.

  • Emergency evacuation procedures
  • Fire safety and drill instructions
  • Campus navigation and room identification

Industrial Facilities

Manufacturing plants, warehouses, and industrial sites require robust safety signage to manage hazards, guide traffic, and ensure worker safety in complex environments.

  • Machinery hazard and warning signs
  • Restricted area and access control signage
  • Chemical safety and GHS labels

Public Venues

Stadiums, airports, and public transit hubs need clear, accessible signage for crowd management, emergency exits, and accessibility compliance to ensure public safety.

  • Crowd control and directional flow signs
  • Accessibility signage (e.g., ramps, elevators)
  • Emergency assembly point and evacuation maps
