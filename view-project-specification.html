2. Material Specifications

Primary Materials

  • Acrylic Sheets: 3mm thickness, UV stable, photoluminescent grade
  • Aluminum Composite: 4mm ACP with PVDF coating
  • LED Modules: IP65 rated, 50,000+ hour lifespan
  • Mounting Hardware: Stainless steel, corrosion resistant

Quality Standards

  • All materials shall comply with relevant Indian Standards (IS)
  • LED modules must have CE/RoHS certification
  • Photoluminescent materials: DIN 67510 Class A
  • Fire resistance: BS 476 Part 7 Class 1

4. Quality Assurance

Testing Requirements

  • Luminance testing for photoluminescent signs
  • Electrical safety testing for illuminated signs
  • Weather resistance testing for outdoor signs
  • Compliance verification with local fire authority
