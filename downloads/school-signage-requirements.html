School Signage Requirements Checklist

Educational Institution Safety & Information Signage Standards
Based on RTE Act 2009, School Safety Guidelines & Educational Standards

Instructions for Use:

  • Scope: Applicable to all educational institutions - Primary, Secondary & Higher Secondary Schools
  • Compliance: Required for RTE compliance, school registration, and safety certifications
  • Critical Items: Red border items are mandatory for student safety and regulatory approval
  • Review: Quarterly assessments recommended with annual comprehensive audits

Age-Specific Signage Requirements:

  • Primary (Ages 6-10): Picture-based signs, bright colors, simple language
  • Middle (Ages 11-13): Clear text with symbols, safety emphasis
  • Secondary (Ages 14-18): Detailed information, advanced safety protocols
  • Universal: Multi-lingual signs where needed, accessibility compliance
