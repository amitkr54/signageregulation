IS 9457:2005 Compliance Requirements

All fire exit signs must comply with IS 9457:2005 standards. Key requirements include green background with white text/symbols, appropriate size ratios, illumination capabilities, and proper mounting heights (2.1-2.4m above floor level).

Installation Guidelines

  • Height: Mount 2.1-2.4m above finished floor level
  • Illumination: Minimum 50 lux at sign face, 3-hour emergency backup
  • Visibility: Unobstructed view from all approach angles
  • Spacing: Maximum 30m between directional signs in corridors
  • Maintenance: Monthly visual inspection, annual electrical testing
  • Documentation: Keep compliance certificates and installation records
