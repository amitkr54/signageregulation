Color Code Standards

  • Red: Danger, prohibition, fire equipment, stop
  • Yellow/Amber: Caution, warning, temporary hazard
  • Blue: Mandatory action, information, PPE requirement
  • Green: Safe condition, emergency escape, first aid
  • Orange: Dangerous parts of machines, energized equipment
  • Purple: Radiation hazard areas and equipment
  • Black/White: Traffic, housekeeping, directional

Installation Guidelines

  • Mount at eye level (1.4-1.7m height) for maximum visibility
  • Ensure adequate lighting (minimum 50 lux) for clear readability
  • Use weather-resistant materials for outdoor applications
  • Position signs to avoid obstruction by equipment or personnel
  • Update signs immediately when hazard conditions change
  • Maintain multilingual signage in diverse workplace environments
  • Regular inspection and replacement of damaged or faded signs
