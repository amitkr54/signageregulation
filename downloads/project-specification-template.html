1. PROJECT SCOPE & OBJECTIVES

1.1 Scope of Work

  • Design, manufacture, and installation of comprehensive signage system
  • Compliance with Indian Standards (IS), NBCC, and local building codes
  • Supply of emergency and safety signage as per IS 9457:2005
  • Directional and wayfinding signage for optimal navigation
  • Corporate identification and branding elements
  • Regulatory and compliance signage installation

1.2 Project Objectives

  • Enhance building navigation and user experience
  • Ensure full compliance with safety regulations
  • Establish consistent brand identity throughout the facility
  • Provide accessible signage for all users including persons with disabilities
  • Deliver durable, low-maintenance signage solutions

Key Requirements

  • All signage must comply with Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016
  • Emergency signage must meet IS 9457:2005 standards
  • Multi-language support where required (English + Local Language)
  • Minimum 10-year material warranty required

3. MATERIAL SPECIFICATIONS

Acrylic (PMMA)
  • Thickness: 3-6mm as required
  • Type: Cast acrylic, UV stabilized
  • Colors: As per brand guidelines
  • Finish: Matte/Gloss as specified
  • Application: Indoor premium signage
Aluminium Composite Panel (ACP)
  • Thickness: 3-4mm standard
  • Core: Polyethylene/Mineral core
  • Skin: 0.3mm aluminium minimum
  • Finish: Powder coated/Anodized
  • Application: Exterior and large format
Stainless Steel
  • Grade: 304/316 as per application
  • Thickness: 1.5-3mm
  • Finish: Brushed/Mirror/Satin
  • Treatment: Laser cut/Etched
  • Application: Premium identification
LED Lighting Systems
  • Type: 12V/24V LED strips
  • Color Temperature: 6500K daylight
  • Life: 50,000 hours minimum
  • IP Rating: IP65 for outdoor use
  • Application: Illuminated signage

Material Compliance Requirements

  • All materials must have fire safety certificates (Class 1 fire rating)
  • UV resistance certification for outdoor applications
  • Environmental compliance (RoHS/REACH where applicable)
  • Structural load calculations for large format installations

5. INSTALLATION REQUIREMENTS

5.1 Mounting Systems

  • Wall Mounting: Stainless steel brackets with chemical anchors for masonry, toggle bolts for drywall
  • Ceiling Suspension: Adjustable cable system with load-rated ceiling attachments
  • Post Mounting: Galvanized steel posts with concrete foundation (600mm depth minimum)
  • Glass Mounting: Structural glazing tape or mechanical fixings as per glass specification

5.2 Electrical Requirements

  • All illuminated signs require separate electrical connections
  • Emergency lighting with 3-hour backup battery systems
  • IP65 rated connections for outdoor installations
  • All electrical work to comply with IS 732 wiring standards
  • Individual switching and control systems where required

Installation Safety Requirements

  • All installation team members must have safety training certificates
  • Use of appropriate PPE mandatory during installation
  • Traffic management plan required for public area work
  • Structural load calculations for all mounting systems
  • Post-installation safety inspections and sign-off

6. QUALITY ASSURANCE & TESTING

6.1 Material Testing

  • UV resistance testing for outdoor materials (ASTM G154)
  • Fire resistance testing as per IS 1644
  • Adhesion testing for graphic applications
  • Color fastness testing (ISO 105)
  • Impact resistance testing for high-traffic areas

6.2 Installation Quality Checks

7. WARRANTY & MAINTENANCE

7.1 Warranty Terms

  • Manufacturing Defects: 2 years from installation completion
  • LED Systems: 1 year or 8,760 hours whichever is earlier
  • Structural Mounting: 5 years for design and installation
  • Material Warranty: As per manufacturer's specifications
  • Exclusions: Physical damage, vandalism, natural disasters

8. COMPLIANCE & STANDARDS

Mandatory Compliance Standards

  • IS 9457:2005 - Safety signs including fire safety signs
  • IS 2190:1992 - Selection, installation and maintenance of automatic fire extinguishing systems
  • NBCC 2016 - National Building Code requirements
  • Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act 2016 - Accessibility requirements
  • IS 732 - Code of practice for electrical wiring installations
  • Local Municipal Regulations - Building and fire safety approvals

8.1 Accessibility Requirements

  • Braille signage for critical areas (elevators, restrooms, emergency exits)
  • High contrast color combinations (minimum 70% contrast ratio)
  • Tactile elements for room identification signs
  • Mounting heights: 1400-1500mm to centerline for accessibility
  • Clear space requirements: minimum 750mm in front of signs
