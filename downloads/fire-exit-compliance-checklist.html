Indian Fire Exit Compliance Checklist

Comprehensive Audit Form for Fire Exit and Emergency Signage
Based on IS 9457:2005, NBCC 2016, BIS Standards & Indian Fire Service Regulations

Instructions for Use:

  • Scope: This checklist covers fire exit signage, emergency lighting, and evacuation route compliance for Indian buildings
  • Frequency: Conduct quarterly inspections for compliance verification
  • Critical Items: Items marked with red border are mandatory for life safety compliance
  • Documentation: Maintain completed checklists for regulatory inspections and insurance requirements

Are exit signs installed above every designated emergency exit door? [NBCC 2016 - Part 4, Section 2.4.1]

Are directional signs placed at maximum 30-meter intervals in corridors? [NBCC 2016 - Part 4, Section 2.4.3]

Are photoluminescent signs compliant with DIN 67510 with sufficient charging periods? [BIS Guidelines 2019]

Is emergency lighting tested monthly with documentation maintained? [Fire Service Regulations]

4. Exit Route Compliance & Accessibility

Yes No
Are all exit routes kept permanently free from obstructions and locked doors? [NBCC 2016 - Part 4, Section 2.2.1]
Yes No
Do exit doors open in direction of egress without keys or special knowledge? [NBCC 2016 - Part 4, Section 2.3.1]

Yes No
Are non-exit doors marked "NOT AN EXIT" or similar appropriate marking? [IS 9457:2005 - Clause 7.2]
