General Safety Signage Audit Checklist

Comprehensive Workplace Safety Communication Assessment
Based on OSH Act, Factory Act 1948, IS Standards & Safety Guidelines

Audit Instructions:

  • Scope: Universal safety signage audit applicable to all workplace environments
  • Frequency: Monthly spot checks, quarterly comprehensive audits recommended
  • Priority Levels: Red = Critical Safety, Orange = High Priority, Regular = Standard Compliance
  • Scoring: Calculate compliance percentage using the score section at the bottom
