📋 Installation & Compliance Guidelines

  • Height Requirements: Exit signs must be mounted 2.1-2.4m above floor level for optimal visibility
  • Spacing Standards: Maximum 30m spacing between exit signs along evacuation routes
  • Illumination: All exit signs must be illuminated with 3-hour battery backup during power failure
  • Visibility Distance: Signs must be clearly visible from 30m distance under normal lighting
  • Material Standards: Use photoluminescent or LED illuminated signs as per IS 9457:2005
  • Multilingual Requirements: Include local language text alongside English in public buildings
  • Regular Maintenance: Monthly inspection and cleaning, annual battery testing required
  • Accessibility Compliance: Include Braille labels for visually impaired as per NBC guidelines
